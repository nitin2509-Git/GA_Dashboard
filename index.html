Expert Action Plan · Car&Bike.com Growth Roadmap
SEO & Organic Traffic
HIGH PRIORITY
Gap: 44% organic vs CarDekho 64% → ~1.5M visits/month left on table
  • Technical audit: Core Web Vitals, schema markup, canonical tags
  • City × model price pages (e.g. "Thar price in Pune") — 10K+ keywords
  • EV-first content: fastest-growing search category, rivals underserving
  • Bike launch pages published within 2hrs of announcement
  • Internal linking overhaul — car → bike → compare → dealer funnel
Engagement & Depth
HIGH PRIORITY
Gap: 2:51 avg session vs CarDekho 4:52 → 2 min of engagement lost per user
  • Embed Car&Bike TV videos on every review & spec page
  • Comparison tool redesign — drives 3–4 extra pageviews per session
  • Related model carousels on every page (like Amazon's "also viewed")
  • EMI calculator embedded in spec pages — reduces drop-off
  • Push notifications for new launches to return traffic
2-Wheeler Offensive
HIGH PRIORITY
BikeDekho leads bike segment at 14.2M — Car&Bike must establish parity
  • Dedicated /bikes section with equal content depth as /cars
  • Scooter hub (high volume, lower competition than motorcycles)
  • EV 2-wheeler section — Ather, Ola S1, TVS iQube are search darlings
  • Bike vs bike comparison pages — 91Wheels weak here, gap to exploit
  • Under-1-lakh budget guide series — commuter segment = highest volume
Mahindra Group Leverage
MEDIUM PRIORITY
No competitor has OEM + editorial + dealer data under one roof
  • Exclusive Mahindra launch content (BE 6, XEV 9, Thar EV) — publish first
  • Dealer network data → real-time stock & availability pages
  • Mahindra Finance integration → in-page loan pre-approval widget
  • Group insurance (Mahindra Insurance Brokers) — monetise buyer intent
  • Pitch OEM advertisers: "We ARE a Mahindra property" = trust signal
Monetisation & Revenue
MEDIUM PRIORITY
Paid traffic at 7% = current growth is expensive; shift mix to organic
  • Reduce paid spend as organic grows — reinvest into content team
  • Launch lead gen product: verified dealer enquiry with SLA scoring
  • Insurance leads: buying-intent page + instant quote widget
  • Accessories & mod marketplace — high margin, untapped by rivals
  • B2B data product: sell anonymised buyer intent data to OEMs
Data & Measurement
FOUNDATION
Can't manage what you don't measure — set baselines first
  • GA4 + BigQuery pipeline for your own reliable traffic data
  • SimilarWeb Pro account — competitive benchmarking monthly
  • Weekly editorial KPI dashboard (organic sessions, ranked pages)
  • Lead quality tracking: enquiry → test drive → purchase conversion
  • Content ROI model: traffic per article, revenue per URL
