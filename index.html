Car&Bike real-data snapshot — key findings from verified SimilarWeb data
Organic is NOT the problem
76.8% organic share — same as CarDekho (77%). The issue is what happens after users land, not how they get there.
Engagement is the crisis
1:09 avg session vs CarDekho 2:30. Users arrive and leave in 60 seconds. Content depth + UX, not SEO, is the fix.
CarWale is actually #1
CarWale is ranked #968 globally vs CarDekho #1,449 — CarWale is the actual market leader, not CarDekho.
India rank gap is brutal
Car&Bike is #3,857 in India vs CarDekho #143. Closing even half that gap would 3× the business.
Expert Action Plan · Revised based on real data
Fix Engagement First
CRITICAL — DO NOW
Real data: 1:09 session, 3.81 pages/visit. Users land and bounce. Revenue is being left on the table every single day.
  • Embed Car&Bike TV videos on every review — extends session by 2–3 minutes
  • Comparison widget redesign: drive 3–4 extra clicks per user
  • "Also check" carousels on spec pages — reduce dead ends
  • EMI calculator on every model page — keeps buyer intent alive
  • Fix mobile page speed — likely the hidden culprit of low engagement
Scale Content Volume
HIGH PRIORITY
Organic share (76.8%) is great. Problem is organic volume — 5.2M vs CarDekho's 30.7M. Need 6× more rankable pages.
  • City × model on-road price pages — 500+ cities × 300+ models = 150K pages
  • Bike vs bike comparison pages — high intent, thin competition
  • EV hub: Ather, Ola, TVS iQube — fastest growing search segment
  • Launch day content: publish specs within 2hrs of announcement
  • Scooter-specific section: under-served vs motorcycles
Close the CarWale Gap
HIGH PRIORITY
New finding: CarWale (#968 global) is bigger than CarDekho (#1,449). Car&Bike must benchmark against both.
  • Study CarWale's content structure — they hold #1 India rank in the category
  • Used car listings: CarWale dominates here, Car&Bike has minimal presence
  • Dealer tool depth: real-time stock, on-road price with RTO included
  • Review length & freshness: CarWale/CarDekho update models faster
  • Finance integration — loan calculators + pre-approval keep users longer
Mahindra Group Leverage
MEDIUM PRIORITY
No competitor can replicate this. OEM access + TV brand + dealer network = compounding advantage.
  • Exclusive Mahindra EV launch content (BE 6, XEV 9, Thar EV)
  • Mahindra First Choice (used cars) referral traffic integration
  • Mahindra Finance widget — loan pre-approval on every model page
  • Dealer inventory data from Mahindra network — real-time availability
  • OEM advertiser pitch: "We ARE a Mahindra property" = trust signal
Monetise Buying Intent
MEDIUM PRIORITY
76.8% organic = high purchase intent traffic. Currently under-monetised vs peers.
